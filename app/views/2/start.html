Local authority maintained schools can apply to become an academy by:

  • joining an existing multi-academy trust
  • forming a new multi-academy trust with other schools
  • becoming a single academy trust

You will be able to invite other people to help, but the declaration section must be completed by the chair of governors or the school's headteacher acting on their behalf.

Before you start

You should:

  • contact your regional schools commissioner's office to discuss your plans
  • have the information and evidence you will need to hand
