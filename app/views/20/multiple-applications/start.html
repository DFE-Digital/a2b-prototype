Apply to become an academy

This form is for local authority maintained schools that want to become academies.

There is a separate process for:

  • special schools, and
  • pupil referral units (PRUs)

Groups of schools can apply together or individually.

Before you start

Applying schools must:

  1. carry out a consultation (opens in new tab) with their stakeholders
  2. complete an Equality Impact Assessment (EIA)
  3. provide bank details (opens in a new tab) - we can pay opening support grants either directly to the school or to the trust it will be part of
