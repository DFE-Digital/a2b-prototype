The application owner

Applications that involve more than one school must have a nominated ‘owner’.

The owner will be responsible for:

  • adding schools to the application
  • providing trust details or inviting somebody else to provide them
  • submitting the application once each school has given its details

Who will be the owner of the application?

You will be the owner of this application until the person you nominate logs in. Afterwards, you will not be able to access this form until you have been invited.
